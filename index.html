01 / About

Full-stack at the core, Power Platform by specialty.

I'm a Senior Software Engineer at Leapfrog Technology, where I've spent four years moving from associate to senior — designing REST and GraphQL APIs in Node.js, building React and PCF components, and architecting Microsoft Power Platform solutions end to end.

My specialty sits where these worlds meet: Canvas Apps and Model-Driven Apps talking to Azure Service Bus, custom connectors syncing with Dataverse, and Publisher–Subscriber patterns that keep large healthcare systems reliable under load.

A good chunk of that work lives in US healthcare integration — connecting state immunization systems like WAIIS and NJIIS, and handling X12 billing transactions (277, 835) where correctness isn't optional.
